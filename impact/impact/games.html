Games

Here you can find the games we are supporting.

League Of Legends

League of Legends is a fast-paced, competitive online game that blends the speed and intensity of an RTS with RPG elements. Two teams of powerful champions, each with a unique design and playstyle, battle head-to-head across multiple battlefields and game modes.

World Of Warcraft

Explore the world of Azeroth, a place of never-ending adventure and action. Experience epic stories and quests. Face deadly dragons, find mythical artifacts, or just visit a nice, quiet corner of the world to do some fishing.

Fifa

Play real football against people with your level, create your own team and beat your friends. Use your favorite team and players thanks to the official license of the Fifa.

Player Unknowns Battlegrounds

Use your survival skills to win the battle in a huge map full of players, with us you will find a partner to survive in the wild lands of Pochinki.

Call Of Duty

Use your skills to become the best soldier in the game, play with your friends and enjoy the victory when you finally get a MOAB.

Rocket League

Do you like playing football and driving cars? This is your game, now the cars play football in this crazy game, find a partner to play and start playing competitive.

Reuniting players from around the world

Do not doubt. You'll find players whose timetable is equal to yours. And they will be willing to help you for sure.
