No matter the game. You can find help in here... or grant it.


Learn More

Our objective

We want to gather all those players that enjoy cooperating with people but don't have any friends to play with.

Need help?

Just make a post asking for help in the section of the game you are playing. And you'll have an army of people willing to help. Wether giving you advices or playing the game with you. If you are satisfied you can upvote those who helped you or even send a friend request to them so you can keep cooperating in the future.

User score

If you help someone solving a problem they may upvote you so the rest of the community knows you are a collaborative member. The more people you help, the higher your score will be. Increasing it one by one may look hard but do not despair. Even the construction of the highest tower always starts by placing the first stone.

But why not play for fun?

You don't always need help. Ask your friends or make a post so people know you are creating a party to play PUBG, for instance.

Log in with Google or Facebook

You can use your Google or Facebook account to log in the site.

Link your accounts

You can link your Steam or Discord accounts to find your friends here.

No matter the platform

Even if you are playing on a console you'll be able to find people willing to help or people to help.

Reuniting players from around the world

Do not doubt. You'll find players whose timetable is equal to yours. And they will be willing to help you for sure.
